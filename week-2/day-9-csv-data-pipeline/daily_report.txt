Daily Report - Day 9 Practice

Today I worked on:
- Building a complete CSV data pipeline using Python.
- Loading raw CSV files and transforming them into clean business-ready data.
- Creating validation, cleaning, enrichment, and reporting logic.

Pipeline parts completed:
- Part 1: Explained the Bronze → Silver → Gold pipeline architecture.
- Part 2: Loaded orders, customers, and products CSV files using csv.DictReader.
- Part 3: Created lookup tables to simulate SQL JOIN operations.
- Part 4: Normalized status, city, and channel values.
- Part 5: Validated orders and separated invalid records with reasons.
- Part 6: Enriched valid orders with customer and product information.

- Part 8: Created data quality report.
- Part 9: Created business summary and Gold reports.

Most difficult part:
- The most difficult part was designing the validation logic and making sure invalid records were not lost.
- Understanding how lookup dictionaries replace SQL JOIN thinking was also challenging.

One bug/error I fixed:
- I fixed a CSV writing error:
  "ValueError: dict contains fields not in fieldnames"
- The issue happened because completed_orders.csv did not include all fields from clean orders.
- I fixed it by updating the CSV writer to ignore extra fields and improving the output field definitions.

What I understood better about Bronze/Silver/Gold:
- Bronze stores raw source data without changing it.
- Silver contains cleaned, validated, and enriched data.
- Gold contains business reports created from trusted Silver data.
- Data should be transformed step by step instead of directly creating reports from raw files.
