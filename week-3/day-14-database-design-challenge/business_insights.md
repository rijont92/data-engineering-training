## 40. Which students are financially risky?

- unpaid payments,
- partial payments,
- active enrollment but incomplete payment history.

These students should be contacted early to prevent payment issues.

## 46. What did your database design make easy, and what was difficult?

### What was easy:
The relational design made reporting easier because tables were connected using primary keys and foreign keys.

Examples:
- connecting students with programs,
- tracking instructor responsibilities,
- calculating revenue,
- analyzing attendance.

### What was difficult:
The most challenging part was designing relationships correctly and ensuring data consistency.

Challenges included:
- handling missing payments,
- tracking multiple enrollments,
- maintaining valid foreign key relationships,
- creating queries that combine multiple tables.

Overall, the database design supports reliable reporting and helps management make better decisions.
